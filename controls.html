Introduction to Feedback Control Course

In this course, I learned how to design PID, State-Space, and Frequency-Domain Controllers on a variety of mechanical systems, both simulated and physical.

The video to the left shows a simulation of a VTOL aircraft with an Integral State Feedback Controller that I designed. This selection of controller has all the advantages of state-space control, with the added benefit of integral action to eliminate steady-state error.

Key skills: Python, control design, git, system dynamics.
